Paper Submission

The Original unpublished Research Papers, Articles & Working papers having maximum length of 8 pages on the topics related to the theme are invited for presentation/ publication in the conference proceedings.

1.Kindly ensure that your paper is formatted as per Elsevier Guidelines (not exceeding 8 pages written in A4 size). Please refer the attached springer template for preparation of your paper.

2. All papers must be submitted online via easychair submission portal.

3. All submissions will be thoroughly peer-reviewed by experts based on originality, significance, and clarity.

4.Only papers presenting original content with novel research results or successful innovative applications will be considered for publication in the conference proceedings (as chapters in edited volumes of Elsevier Series).
